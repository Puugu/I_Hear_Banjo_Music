FAQ's!

Page to ask a question to find a similar question we assumed one might ask to see if you can answer the question you asked which is not exactly the question in which you find your answer.

Question: Does this app keep track of the racers on the race route?

Answer: No. This app is for strictly registration/registration management, race timing, and the display of results.

Question: Will this app make it easier to sign up for my local paddle boat racing competition?

Answer: Heck yeah! Sign up for a race in a matter of minutes on your web browser enabled device. Bypass that annoying registration paperwork on race day.

Question: As a race director, how will this improve my experience while managing an event?

Answer: Paddle Racer is your one-stop-shop for registration, timing, and results.

Question: What makes this app different from those currently available?

Answer: It’s simple to use and it’s free!

Question: Does this app make it easier for an event organizer to keep track of participants?

Answer: Sure thing! The event organizer can confirm current registrations with the click of a button.
